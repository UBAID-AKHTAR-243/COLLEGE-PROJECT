COVID-19 PANDEMIC
CORONA VIRUS STILL EXISTS.
The health and safety of all who study and work here is our highest priority. Staff, students, visitors, contractors, and members of the public will be required to use face coverings across all college buildings as set out in the face coverings policy. A face covering is typically a cloth covering for the nose and mouth. Social distancing at 2m remains a key feature of the hygiene and safety measures that the college has in place. Face coverings are not a substitute for this and other hygiene and safety measures, such as being tested when displaying symptoms, self-isolating when unwell or advised to, enhanced cleaning regimes, and regular hand-washing. These primary mitigation measures should always be followed.
